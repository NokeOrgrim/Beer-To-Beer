History of our beers

Welcome to one of Belgium's best breweries, where our slogan "as much beers as friends" captures the heart of our mission: to bring people together over the finest beers. Our journey is a testament to the rich tradition of Belgian brewing, blended with a commitment to community and camaraderie.

Beginnings: A Dream Fermented

Our story begins in the early 1990s, when a group of friends with a passion for craft beer decided to turn their shared hobby into a professional endeavor. Inspired by Belgium's illustrious brewing heritage, they embarked on a mission to create beers that would not only stand out for their quality but also foster a sense of community.

Crafting the First Brew

In 1895, our founders established the brewery in a quaint, historical building in the heart of Belgium. The location was chosen for its rich history and perfect brewing conditions. The first batch, a classic Rocky Mountain , was brewed using traditional methods and local ingredients. It was an instant success, celebrated not just for its flavor but for the warmth and friendship it brought to those who enjoyed it.

Expansion and Innovation

As word spread, our brewery grew. By the early 2000s, we had expanded our facilities and introduced new brewing technologies, while still adhering to the artisanal methods that made our beers special. We experimented with different styles, from robust Tripels to refreshing Witbiers, ensuring that there was a beer for every palate.

Community and Camaraderie

True to our slogan, we have always believed that beer is best enjoyed with friends. Over the years, we have hosted countless events, from local festivals to intimate tasting sessions, all designed to bring people together. Our brewery tours offer a behind-the-scenes look at our process, allowing visitors to share in the magic of beer-making.

The Beer-to-Beer Community

In recent years, we have taken our commitment to community a step further by creating the "beer-to-beer" community and team. This initiative connects beer enthusiasts from around the world, fostering a global network of friends united by their love for great beer. Through online forums, collaborative brewing projects, and international events, we continue to build bridges and strengthen bonds.
