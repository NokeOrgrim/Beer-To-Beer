History of our beers

Blackout Stout - A Dark and Rich Brew

Originating in 19th century industrial England as a hearty sustenance for hardworking laborers, Blackout Stout has evolved into a symbol of craftsmanship and tradition in the craft beer world. Its robust flavor and high alcohol content made it a favorite among workers, gaining notoriety during the Prohibition era in the United States as a prized commodity on the black market. Today, brewed with the finest roasted malts, hops, and yeast, each batch preserves its rich heritage while embracing innovation. With its velvety texture, notes of chocolate and coffee, and warming alcohol finish, Blackout Stout remains a beloved brew cherished by all who appreciate the finer things in life.

Rocky Mountain - A Refreshing Alpine Brew

Born amidst the rugged peaks of the Rocky Mountains, this refreshing ale embodies the spirit of adventure and pristine wilderness. Crafted by a small mountain brewery nestled in the heart of Colorado, Rocky Mountain beer captures the essence of its surroundings with every sip. Its crisp, clean taste and subtle hints of pine and citrus evoke images of crystal-clear streams and pine-scented air. Popular among outdoor enthusiasts and beer connoisseurs alike, Rocky Mountain has become a staple for those seeking refreshment after a day of exploration in the great outdoors. Whether enjoyed at a mountaintop vista or shared around a campfire under a starry sky, Rocky Mountain beer brings the essence of the Rockies to life in every bottle.

Tunnal Rat - A Bold and Courageous Brew

Conceived in the trenches of World War I, Tunnel Rat beer embodies the bravery and camaraderie of soldiers facing the harsh realities of war. Originally brewed by troops seeking solace and fortitude in the depths of underground tunnels, this bold and courageous brew quickly gained a reputation for its robust flavor and invigorating qualities. As soldiers carried the recipe with them through conflicts around the globe, Tunnel Rat became a symbol of resilience and unity in the face of adversity. Today, brewed with the same determination and spirit of its origins, Tunnel Rat beer honors the sacrifice and valor of those who served, providing a moment of strength and solidarity with every sip. Whether enjoyed in a local pub or raised in tribute to fallen comrades, Tunnel Rat beer remains a testament to the indomitable spirit of the human heart.

Skyrim's Hydromel - A Mythic Elixir from the Northern Realms

Crafted in the mythical land of Skyrim, Hydromel embodies the ancient traditions and mystique of Nordic culture. Inspired by the rich lore and rugged landscapes of the northern realms, this golden elixir combines the sweetness of honey with the warmth of spices to create a truly enchanting brew. With each sip, adventurers are transported to the halls of Nordic mead halls, where tales of heroism and valor echo through the ages. Whether enjoyed by the hearth on a frosty evening or shared among comrades after a hard-fought battle, Skyrim's Hydromel evokes the spirit of adventure and discovery that defines the land of the Nords. As a tribute to the legends of Skyrim and the boundless wonders of its world, this mythical elixir continues to captivate and inspire all who dare to raise a horn in its honor.
